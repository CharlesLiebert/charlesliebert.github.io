Mesmerized by the mystical harmony of Jimi Hendrix and the rebel rock of Alex Zhang Hungtai. Charles Liebert and his cyberpunk unfold with his Neon Rock, well reflected on his album The Neon Dreams of Electric Sirens; psychedelic keyboards, mystical guitars and a furious voice that tells stories of battles that have not yet happened. From Mexico to Neo-Tokyo, Charles Liebert brings psychedelia, rock'n'roll and rebellion since 2019 ...
